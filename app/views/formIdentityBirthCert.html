Birth certificate details

Must be issued in the UK, Isle of Man, or Channel Islands. This includes those issued by UK authorities overseas, for example embassies, High Commissions, and HM Forces.

If your birth certificate is not in your current name, you will need to take a supporting legal document to the Post Office that proves your name change. This could be, for example, a marriage certificate or a deed poll document.
