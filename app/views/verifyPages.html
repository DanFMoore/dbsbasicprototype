Validating your identity

Before getting a criminal record check you need to validate your identity. You can do this online for free using GOV.UK Verify or a digital identity from another European country.

Other ways to apply

If you cannot validate your identity online, you can continue to apply. You still need your financial and identity documents to hand.

But you'll also need to go to a UK post office in person after completing your application.

You'll have 14 days to take your identity documents to a UK post office that offers the DBS ID validation service.

We'll only start processing your application after you’ve validated your identity at a UK post office.
