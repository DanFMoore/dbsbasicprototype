Verifying your identity at the Post Office

To verify your identity at the Post Office you will need to:

1. Select 3 identity documents to take with you.

2. Print off the page with barcodes we'll generate when you submit your application.

3. Take your documents and the barcodes to your nearest Post Office identity checking service.

Check which Post Office near you offers an identity checking service.

You have 28 days after submitting your application to verify your identity at the Post Office. If you don't, the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) will delete your application and refund your application fee.
