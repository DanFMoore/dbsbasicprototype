Apply for a basic criminal record check

A basic DBS check (formerly a CRB check) shows unspent cautions and unspent convictions.

Eligibility

There is no eligibility criteria for a basic DBS check but you must be 16 or over.
There are different rules for getting a criminal record check in Scotland and Northern Ireland.

For certain roles you may need a standard or enhanced DBS check. Use the criminal record check tool to find out what type of DBS check is right for you. You can contact DBS if you’re not sure.

Cost

A basic DBS check costs £16

What you need to get started

To verify your identity you'll need to set up an account with GOV.UK Verify or log in to an existing account.

You'll also need:
  • your National Insurance number
  • 5 year address history
  • a valid Passport or a valid driving licence

It'll take you around 15 minutes to set up a GOV.UK Verfiy account, and another 15 minutes to apply for a basic DBS check. You don't need to do these at the same time.

Verifying your identity

You'll need to verify your identity using GOV.UK Verify.
To use GOV.UK Verify you'll need a UK passport, photocard driving licence or bank statement.
