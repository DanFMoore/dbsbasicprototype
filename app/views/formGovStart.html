Apply for a basic criminal record check

Use this service to apply for a basic criminal record check. Your application will be checked against the police national computer and you'll get a certificate that says one of the following:

  • you don't have any convictions
  • what current (unspent) convictions you have

Eligibility

Anyone over 16 can apply, unless you:

  • live in Scotland or Northern Ireland
  • havve been asked to get a standard or enhanced DBS check for a job

Ask your employer if you're not sure which type of criminal record check you need to get for a job.

How much it costs

It costs Â£16 to get a basic criminal record check.

What you will need

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • details of all your addresses for the last 5 years - including the dates you lived there
  • a debit or credit card to pay online

You also need the following, if you have them:

  • national insurance number
  • valid UK passport
  • valid UK driving licence
