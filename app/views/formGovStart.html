Apply for a basic criminal record check

Use the basic criminal record check service to find out what unspent convictions and cautions you have. (This is the same as a Basic Disclosure from Disclosure Scotland).

You can't use this service if:

  • you're under 16
  • you live in Scotland or Northern Ireland
  • you've been asked to get a standard or enhanced DBS check for your job

Ask your employer if you're not sure which type of criminal record check you need to get for a job.

What you will need

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • details of all your addresses for the last 5 years - including the dates you lived there
  • your national insurance number
  • a valid passport or driving licence
  • a debit or credit card to pay online
