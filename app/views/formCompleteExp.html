What to do next

You must validate your identity by {{ date }}.

  • we will send you an email with a link to a letter you need to print (always check your spam or junk folders if you haven't received an email)
  • you must then take the letter and the 3 documents you selected (original versions, not photocopies) to a post office that has the DBS ID validation service
  • we will then notify you by email when your certificate has been issued and tell you the result of your check
  • your certificate will then be sent to you by post

Not all post offices have the DBS ID validation service. You can use our branch finder to search for post offices near you that do.

After you have validated your identity at the Post Office, you don't need to do anything else.

You will receive your application reference number by email after you have validated your identity at a post office. It could take up to 3 days for your email to arrive.
