What to do next

You must validate your identity by 19 July 2018.

  • we will send you an email with a link to a letter you need to print
  • you must then take the letter and the 3 documents you selected (original versions, not photocopies) to a post office that has the 'DBS ID validation service'

Not all post offices offer the DBS ID validation service. You can find your nearest DBS ID validation service on the Post Office website: http://www.postoffice.co.uk/branch-finder.

After entering your location you must select 'refine branch services' and choose 'DBS ID validation service' from the list.
